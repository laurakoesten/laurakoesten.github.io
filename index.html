I am an Assistant Professor at the Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), UAE. I am also affiliated with the University of Vienna (Research Group for Visualization and Data Analysis, Faculty of Computer Science) and a Senior Scientist at the AIT Austrian Institute of Technology, Austria.

My research focuses on human-data interaction - how people make sense of data, how they are discovered and used, and the ethical and collaborative dimensions of data-centric work, including data governance and explainable AI. I’m particularly interested in how different groups, from general audiences to domain experts, engage with data and what this means for understanding and communication. Building on this, I develop and study methods and tools that make data and models more understandable for diverse audiences.
